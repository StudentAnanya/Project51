Interesting facts about Panda...

  1. Pandas have 6 toes to grasp bamboos.
  2. Pandas go from pink to white and black(or brown).
  3. An adult panda can eat 12-38 kilos of bamboo per day!

Chameleon

Interesting facts about Chameleon...

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin colour is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Flying Cicada

Interesting facts about Flying Cicada...

  • The scientific name of flying cicada is Cicadoidea.
  • The size of a flying cicada is almost of a paper clip i.e. 0.75 to 2.25 inches.
  • Average life span of a flying cicada is up to 17 years.
